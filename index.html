About

A quieter, more personal approach to skincare.

Lana Society was created by Alana, a licensed esthetician with a passion for helping people feel confident and comfortable in their own skin. After seeing how intimidating skincare can feel, she wanted to create an experience that felt welcoming, simple, and personalized — a place where anyone could feel at ease, whether it's their first facial or part of their regular routine.

Every treatment is thoughtfully chosen based on your skin's needs and goals. With honest guidance, personalized care, and a calm private setting, each appointment is designed to help you better understand your skin and leave feeling refreshed, cared for, and confident.

A Calm, Private Experience
One client at a time. A quiet space where you can fully relax without feeling rushed.
Simple, Honest Guidance
You'll always understand what your skin needs, why a treatment is recommended, and how to care for it moving forward.
Skincare With Purpose
Every treatment and enhancement has a reason behind it — focused on supporting your skin, not adding unnecessary steps.
A Place For Everyone
Whether you're new to facials or already invested in your skincare, Lana Society is a comfortable place to begin.
